Nova

Documentation

Overview: Nova is a versatile learning assistant suite designed to enhance your experience on educational platforms like Big Ideas Math and Achieve3000. It automatically analyzes questions and offers intelligent suggestions for various question formats.

Features:

  • Automatic question analysis and answer suggestions
  • Supports multiple question formats (multiple choice, True/False, fill-in-the-blank)
  • Intelligent assistance for Achieve3000 assignments
  • Easy integration via a bookmarklet
  • Clean and user-friendly interface

Installation: To get started, simply drag the bookmarklet above to your bookmarks bar. Then, when visiting a supported site, click the bookmarklet to activate Nova.
